Africa in the World

The site provides a high level view of the Africa continent in terms of Land Size, Population and Gross Domestic Product (GDP) relative to the Rest of the World.

There are 54 countries in the geographic continent of Africa, of which 6 (Algeria, Djibouti, Egypt, Libya, Morocco and Tunisia) are categorised economically by the World Bank within the "Middle East and North Africa" sector, and 48 in the "Sub-Saharan Africa" sector.
