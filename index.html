Africa in the World

The site provides a high level view of the Africa continent in terms of Land Size, Population and Gross Domestic Product relative to the World.

There are 54 countries, of which 6 (Algeria, Djibouti, Egypt, Libya, Morocco and Tunisia) are categorised economically by the World Bank within the "Middle East and North Africa" sector, and the remainder in the "Sub-Saharan Africa" sector.
