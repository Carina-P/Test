Africa in the World

The site intends to provide a high level view of the Africa continent in terms of Land Size, Population and Gross Domestic Product relative to the World.

There are 54 countries, of which 6 (Algeria, Djibouti, Egypt, Libya, Morocco and Tunisia) are often categorised within the "Middle East and North Africa" sector, and the remainder as "Sub-Saharan Africa".

In relative terms the Africa continent positions itself in a World context as follows;

  • Land area (sq. km): 32.4 Million / 137.3 Million (24%).
  • Population: 1,527 Million / 7,591 Million (20%).
  • Gross Domestic Product (GDP): US$Bn 5,327 / US$Bn 86,408 (6%).
